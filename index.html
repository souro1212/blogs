Application Security for developer

Dec 12, 2020
Security AppSec

In today's digital landscape, where software vulnerabilities and data breaches are becoming increasingly prevalent, ensuring the security of applications is of paramount importance. Application Security, commonly known as AppSec, focuses on protecting software from threats and vulnerabilities throughout its development lifecycle. While organizations typically have dedicated security teams, developers also play a crucial role in building secure applications. In this blog post, we will explore some essential practices and principles for developers to enhance AppSec in their projects.
